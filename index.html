Biography

YINGJIE ZHOU is a soprano with a rich background in operatic performance. She holds a Master of Music in Vocal Performance from the Manhattan School of Music (2013–2015).

Her performance credits include Zerlina in Mozart's Don Giovanni with Amore Opera (2023–2024), and participation in the Elf Ensemble for La Campana Sommersa at the Rose Theater at Jazz at Lincoln Center with New York City Opera (2017).
